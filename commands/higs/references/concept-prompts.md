# Cinematic Ad Hook Concept Seeds — Jay Feldman / Lead Gen Jay

15 visual-metaphor seeds for the `/higs` brainstorm sub-routine. Each is a self-contained scene Jay is inside. NO talking-to-camera. NO smiles unless a seed flags an exception. 85mm lens, shallow DoF, photoreal. Pair with anti-slop negatives from `higs-config.json`.

**Hand-gesture rule per config:** "phone tap" and "phone in palm pulses" are canonical brand-signature beats and exempt from the no-hand-gestures rule. All other specific gestures (pointing, thumbs up, finger snap, hand wave) remain banned unless explicitly requested in the brief.

Pull 2-3 relevant seeds via the topic index at the bottom. Compose with KB pain-points and `scraped_ads` top performers.

## Seed 1: Cold-caller graveyard
**Pain:** Manual phone outreach is dead, reps burn out, nobody answers.
**Scaffold:** Lemlist "old way" visualization, Instantly dashboard reveal.
**Visual:** Wide dusk shot. Jay walks slowly through a graveyard, headstones glow faint red, each etched with a phone number. He stops mid-frame and the phone in his palm pulses pink. Every headstone simultaneously flips to clean white marble reading "REPLIED" in serif font. Cold blue fog dissipates into golden light. 9s.
**Model fit:** Seedance 2.0.
**ABCD beat:** Attention.

## Seed 2: Inbox burial

The avalanche of paper instantly transforms into clean glowing emails that float upward through the beam into a single tidy inbox. 9s.

## Composition rules (for /higs brainstorm sub-routine)

When the brainstorm step composes 3 final concepts for the user:

1. Pull seeds matching the topic via the topic index above.
2. Adapt the seed text to the specific KB pain-point quote retrieved. Replace generic pain wording with the actual phrase from KB.
3. Match the seed's "Model fit" to the routing config; visual-metaphor seeds default to Seedance 2.0.
4. Each output concept MUST include: scene description (1-2 sentences), pain it maps to, model that will be used, estimated cost, ABCD beat.
5. NEVER output more than 3 concepts. Force ranking.
6. Pink-pulse / pink-flare beats are signature, NOT mandatory. If a topic doesn't naturally call for one, don't force it. Alternatives: golden light wash, lens flash, focus rack, slow dissolve, camera push-in reveal.
