# Meta Ads Pro System — phase gate prompts

Exact user-facing copy for each gate. Reference as `§P{n}.{tag}`. Keep the voice plain,
confident, results-focused. No emoji, no em-dashes.

---

## §P0.new
> Let's build your Meta ads system from scratch. First, a name for this account/build (a slug
> like `acme-coaching`) and the offer you're advertising (what someone buys, and the price).

## §P0.resume
> You have an in-progress Meta Ads build. Want to resume it, or start a new one? Resuming picks up
> at the first unfinished phase.

---

## §P1.intro
> Phase 1 of 7 — Tracking. Before we spend a dollar, we install the signal Meta's algorithm
> optimizes against: your Meta Pixel plus the server-side Conversions API, deduplicated so they
> never double-count. This is what makes every later phase profitable. You do NOT need Hyros or any
> other tool. I'll hand you to the tracking setup now; it'll ask for your Pixel ID, a Conversions
> API token, your ad account, Page, and domain.

## §P2.intro
> Phase 2 of 7 — Strategy. Now we decide who to target and what to say. Tell me: the offer, your
> ideal customer, any competitors or current ads you like, and a rough monthly budget. I'll research
> angles and build a brief your campaigns inherit. (No research budget? We can set the ICP and three
> angles directly.)

## §P3.intro
> Phase 3 of 7 — Audiences. With tracking live, we build your targeting inventory: lookalikes seeded
> on your best customers for cold prospecting, plus retargeting audiences (site visitors, video
> viewers, engagers, your CRM list) split by how recent and engaged they are. Buyers always get
> excluded from cold. I'll tell you which audiences already exist and which we create.

## §P4.intro
> Phase 4 of 7 — Creative. Each angle from your strategy becomes an on-brand ad creative, sized for
> Meta placements. We keep Meta's auto "enhancements" OFF so nothing drifts off-brand. You'll review
> and approve each one before it ever enters a campaign.

## §P5.intro
> Phase 5 of 7 — Build. I'll create the campaign, ad sets, and ads in your account, optimized toward
> the purchase/lead conversion we set up in Phase 1. Everything is built PAUSED and run through a
> pre-flight checklist (URL reachable, tracking wired, budget math, audience sizes). Nothing spends
> yet.

## §P6.intro
> Phase 6 of 7 — Launch. Here's the full plan and the total daily spend:
> {plan_summary}
> Total: ${total_per_day}/day across {adset_count} ad sets.
> Reply "launch" to take it live. Anything else and we keep it paused.

## §P7.intro
> Phase 7 of 7 — Optimize. Your ads are live. Give it 48-72 hours to gather data before we cut
> anything. From here I review on a cadence (weekly is a good default): kill what loses, scale what
> wins, judged on real conversions from your tracking, never raw clicks. Want me to set a weekly
> optimization + report rhythm?

## §final
> Your Meta Ads Pro System is live and set to improve over time. From here:
> - Re-run optimization weekly (sooner if you're spending aggressively).
> - Pull a deep report any time, and run a full account audit periodically.
> - Keep your Event Match Quality green and re-verify tracking after any funnel change.
> - Scale proven winners; let new tests discover the next ones.
