## Mode 4: Calling Out BS

### Fake Gurus (from interview)
> Do you know those entrepreneurs you see on Instagram flashing their supercars and Rolexes? Chances are they are trying to sell you a course on Amazon automation or some fake way they got rich, whereas in reality, they are only getting rich because they tricked you into buying their course.
>
> Follow this rule and you'll be okay: Don't buy a course because of what it promises to do for you. Buy it because you love the creator's free content.

### Cold Email Reality (from 2026 nurture)
> Let's face it: cold emails will annoy most prospects.
>
> But there's a simple way to go from being a pesky inbox mosquito to getting genuine replies.
>
> Most people go straight for the hard sell.
>
> But your prospects are busy and don't trust strangers yet.

### AI Personalization BS (from 2026 nurture)
> Most AI personalization gets your prospect rolling their eyes... So let me show you how to do it right.
>
> Run the bullsh*t test. Before sending anything, ask yourself: if you received this email, would you think a human wrote it? If not, don't send it.
