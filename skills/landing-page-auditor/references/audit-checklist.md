# Landing Page Audit Checklist

Comprehensive 50+ point checklist for systematic landing page audits.

---

## Above-Fold Audit (25 points)

### Headline (8 points)
- [ ] Headline is visible without scrolling (2)
- [ ] Headline clearly states the value proposition (2)
- [ ] Headline addresses the reader directly (1)
- [ ] Headline uses specific numbers or results (1)
- [ ] Headline creates curiosity or urgency (1)
- [ ] Headline length is appropriate (not too long) (1)

### Subheadline (4 points)
- [ ] Subheadline provides supporting detail (2)
- [ ] Subheadline explains HOW or WHY (1)
- [ ] Subheadline is readable and clear (1)

### Hero Visual (4 points)
- [ ] Hero image is relevant to the offer (2)
- [ ] Image quality is professional (1)
- [ ] Image doesn't distract from CTA (1)

### Primary CTA (6 points)
- [ ] CTA is visible without scrolling (2)
- [ ] CTA button stands out visually (2)
- [ ] CTA copy is action-oriented (1)
- [ ] CTA explains what happens next (1)

### Trust Indicator (3 points)
- [ ] At least one trust element above fold (1)
- [ ] Customer count, rating, or logo visible (1)
- [ ] Trust element is credible, not generic (1)

---

## Copy & Messaging Audit (20 points)

### Problem Articulation (5 points)
- [ ] Problem is clearly stated (2)
- [ ] Problem is specific, not generic (1)
- [ ] Reader can identify with the problem (1)
- [ ] Consequences of problem are shown (1)

### Solution Presentation (5 points)
- [ ] Solution is clearly explained (2)
- [ ] Unique mechanism or approach is highlighted (1)
- [ ] Solution connects directly to problem (1)
- [ ] Solution seems achievable (1)

### Benefits vs Features (5 points)
- [ ] Benefits are emphasized over features (2)
- [ ] Benefits are specific, not vague (1)
- [ ] Benefits answer "what's in it for me?" (1)
- [ ] Benefits use bullet points for scanning (1)

### Copy Quality (5 points)
- [ ] Copy is conversational, not corporate (1)
- [ ] Copy addresses reader as "you" (1)
- [ ] Copy length matches offer complexity (1)
- [ ] No grammar/spelling errors (1)
- [ ] Copy is scannable with headers/bullets (1)

---

## CTA & Conversion Path Audit (20 points)

### Primary CTA (8 points)
- [ ] Only ONE primary CTA is promoted (2)
- [ ] CTA button uses contrasting color (2)
- [ ] CTA copy uses action verb (1)
- [ ] CTA is repeated throughout page (1)
- [ ] CTA button is appropriately sized (1)
- [ ] CTA has supporting micro-copy (1)

### Form Optimization (6 points)
- [ ] Form asks for minimum required fields (2)
- [ ] Form fields have clear labels (1)
- [ ] Required fields are indicated (1)
- [ ] Form has no unnecessary friction (1)
- [ ] Submit button is clear (1)

### Conversion Path (6 points)
- [ ] Next step after CTA is clear (2)
- [ ] No competing navigation/CTAs (2)
- [ ] Page focuses on single objective (1)
- [ ] Exit intent is considered (1)

---

## Trust & Social Proof Audit (15 points)

### Testimonials (5 points)
- [ ] Testimonials are present (1)
- [ ] Testimonials include specific results (2)
- [ ] Testimonials have names and credentials (1)
- [ ] Testimonials are relevant to audience (1)

### Case Studies/Results (4 points)
- [ ] Results or outcomes are shown (2)
- [ ] Numbers are specific, not rounded (1)
- [ ] Timeframes are included (1)

### Trust Badges (3 points)
- [ ] Security badges are present (1)
- [ ] Payment logos are visible (1)
- [ ] Professional certifications shown (1)

### Risk Reversal (3 points)
- [ ] Guarantee is offered (1)
- [ ] Guarantee is clearly explained (1)
- [ ] Guarantee reduces perceived risk (1)

---

## Design & UX Audit (10 points)

### Visual Hierarchy (3 points)
- [ ] Eye naturally flows down the page (1)
- [ ] Most important elements are most prominent (1)
- [ ] White space is used effectively (1)

### Typography (3 points)
- [ ] Body text is readable (16px+ on desktop) (1)
- [ ] Headlines use appropriate font weights (1)
- [ ] Line spacing allows for easy reading (1)

### Mobile Experience (4 points)
- [ ] Page is fully responsive (2)
- [ ] CTA is easily tappable on mobile (1)
- [ ] Form is usable on mobile (1)

---

## Technical Performance Audit (10 points)

### Page Speed (4 points)
- [ ] Page loads in under 3 seconds (2)
- [ ] Images are optimized (1)
- [ ] No render-blocking resources (1)

### Functionality (4 points)
- [ ] All links work correctly (1)
- [ ] Form submits successfully (2)
- [ ] No JavaScript errors (1)

### Analytics & Tracking (2 points)
- [ ] Analytics is properly installed (1)
- [ ] Conversion tracking is set up (1)

---

## Additional Conversion Factors

### Urgency & Scarcity
- [ ] Real urgency element present (deadline, limited quantity)
- [ ] Scarcity is genuine, not fake
- [ ] Countdown timer if applicable

### Objection Handling
- [ ] FAQ section addresses common concerns
- [ ] Pricing objections are addressed
- [ ] "Why now" is answered
- [ ] "Why you" is answered

### Page Architecture
- [ ] Logical flow from problem to solution to CTA
- [ ] No dead ends or confusing navigation
- [ ] Footer doesn't distract from conversion

### Accessibility
- [ ] Alt text on images
- [ ] Sufficient color contrast
- [ ] Keyboard navigable

---

## Scoring Guide

### Per Item Scoring
- **2 points**: Item is excellent/fully optimized
- **1 point**: Item is present but could be improved
- **0 points**: Item is missing or poorly executed

### Category Calculation
Sum points in each category, then weight:
- Above-Fold: Sum × 1.0 (max 25)
- Copy & Messaging: Sum × 1.0 (max 20)
- CTA & Conversion: Sum × 1.0 (max 20)
- Trust & Social Proof: Sum × 1.0 (max 15)
- Design & UX: Sum × 1.0 (max 10)
- Technical: Sum × 1.0 (max 10)

**Total: Sum of all categories (max 100)**

---

## Red Flags (Automatic Critical Issue)

If ANY of these are true, flag as Critical regardless of overall score:

1. **No visible CTA** above the fold
2. **Page load time** over 5 seconds
3. **Broken form** that doesn't submit
4. **No trust signals** anywhere on page
5. **Mobile completely broken** (unreadable/unusable)
6. **Headline is generic** ("Welcome" or company name only)
7. **Multiple competing CTAs** confusing the visitor
8. **Form asks for credit card** before establishing value

## Comparative Benchmarks

### Industry Average Conversion Rates
- Lead Gen Landing Pages: 2-5%
- E-commerce Product Pages: 1-3%
- SaaS Free Trial: 3-7%
- Webinar Registration: 20-40%
- Email Opt-in: 1-5%

### Performance Benchmarks
- Page Load: Under 3s (good), Under 2s (excellent)
- Bounce Rate: Under 40% (good), Under 30% (excellent)
- Mobile Traffic: Usually 50-70% of visitors

Use these to contextualize recommendations.
