## Testimonial Templates

```
If you're [target audience] dealing with [problem], stop wasting time on [alternatives]. [Solution] actually works."

— [Name], [Title/Company]
```

"Six months ago, I was struggling to get consistent leads for my coaching practice. I had tried Facebook ads and LinkedIn outreach, but the leads were either unqualified or completely cold.

Then I discovered Lead Gen Jay and everything changed. Within 30 days, I had 12 qualified calls booked. Then I closed 4 new clients at $3,500 each. And now I have a waitlist for the first time ever.

If you're a coach dealing with inconsistent lead flow, stop wasting time on generic marketing. Lead Gen Jay actually works."

— Sarah Mitchell, Executive Coach at Elevate Leadership

## Case Study Templates

### Short Case Study (300 words)

```
## [Client Name/Company]: [One-Line Result]

### The Challenge
[Client name] is a [description] who was struggling with [specific problem]. Despite trying [previous attempts], they [consequence of failure].

### The Solution
When [client] connected with [your company], we [specific approach]. This included:
- [Action 1]
- [Action 2]
- [Action 3]

### The Results
Within [timeframe], [client] achieved:
- **[Metric 1]:** [specific number/result]
- **[Metric 2]:** [specific number/result]
- **[Metric 3]:** [specific number/result]

### In Their Words
"[Testimonial quote about the experience and transformation]"
— [Name], [Title]
```

```
1. [Insight relevant to prospects with similar challenges]
2. [Another applicable insight]
3. [Call-to-action adjacent insight]

**Ready to achieve results like [client name]?** [CTA]
```

## Social Proof Placement Checklist

### Hero Section
- [ ] Customer count or impressive stat
- [ ] Star rating with review count
- [ ] "Trusted by [notable company]" or logo

### After Problem Section
- [ ] Testimonial matching the main pain point
- [ ] Should start with "I was struggling with..."

### After Solution Section
- [ ] Case study showing mechanism works
- [ ] Numbers proving the approach

### Before Each CTA
- [ ] Trust badges (security, guarantee)
- [ ] Quick testimonial or result

### Sidebar/Floating
- [ ] Recent activity ("John from NYC just signed up")
- [ ] Review aggregate ("4.9/5 from 847 reviews")

### Footer
- [ ] Full logo strip of clients
- [ ] Certifications and memberships
- [ ] Years in business

## Interview Questions for Gathering Testimonials

### Opening Questions
1. "What was your situation before working with us?"
2. "What made you decide to try our solution?"
3. "What other solutions had you tried?"

### Experience Questions
4. "What was your first impression?"
5. "What surprised you most about working with us?"
6. "What was the experience like?"

### Results Questions
7. "What specific results have you achieved?"
8. "How long did it take to see results?"
9. "What's the ROI been for you?"

### Transformation Questions
10. "How has this changed your business/life?"
11. "What can you do now that you couldn't before?"
12. "What would you say to someone considering this?"

### The Magic Question
13. "If you had to summarize your experience in one sentence, what would it be?"

## Quick Reference: Proof Type by Objection

| Objection | Best Proof Type | Template to Use |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| "Too expensive" | ROI testimonial | Specific Number |
| "Won't work for me" | Industry case study | Short Case Study |
| "Tried before" | Skeptic testimonial | The Skeptic |
| "Takes too long" | Quick win testimonial | Quick Win |
| "Not ready yet" | Transformation story | The Transformation |
| "Not legitimate" | Trust badges + media | Credentials Bar |
