## Objection Handling

Use these in Jay's voice: direct, empathetic, proof-backed. No corporate hedging.

### "I'm not technical / I don't know how to code"

> "You don't need to be. No CS degree, no Stack Overflow, no YouTube rabbit holes. AIA teaches you to build real tools using plain English commands. And if you want the whole system built for you — that's what The Machine is for. Either way, the technical stuff is handled."

**Route to:** AIA (DIY) or The Machine (DFY)

---

### "I've been burned by other courses or agencies before"

> "Same. I've personally spent money on 100+ courses that overpromised and underdelivered. That's exactly why I built mine differently. Insiders has a 14-day refund. AIA has a 30-day no-questions-asked guarantee. The Machine has a KPI-based warranty — if the system doesn't hit industry-standard reply rates, you get a full refund. I put my money where my mouth is."

**Route to:** Lead Gen Insiders or The Machine depending on budget

---

### "I don't have the time to learn a new system"

> "Lifetime membership means no rush. Log in when you're ready, rewatch anything, stay at your pace. And if time is genuinely the constraint — The Machine eliminates 20+ hours a week of manual prospecting. You don't need to learn. You just need to feed it leads."

**Route to:** Lead Gen Insiders (mention lifetime access) or The Machine (DFY angle)

---

### "Will this work for my industry?"

> "Roofing, medical education, SaaS, nonprofit, staffing, consulting, fintech, real estate — we've run campaigns across all of them. Industry-specific KPI baselines exist for The Machine's warranty for a reason. If you're sending to businesses, cold email works. The question is whether your system is built right."

**Route to:** Case studies section, then appropriate offer based on budget

---

### "I hate sales calls"

> "Totally fair. For The Machine, there's a no-call option — you'll get a 3-minute video breakdown of the entire system with no pressure. Everything else is self-serve. Pick a tier, start immediately, no Zoom required."

**Route to:** No-call video path (The Machine) or self-serve offers (Insiders, AIA, Consulti.ai)

---

### "I can't afford it right now"

> "Start free. Consulti.ai gives you 1,000 leads at zero cost. Insiders has financing from $97/month — most people recoup that in their first booked call. AIA is a subscription you can cancel if it's not working. There's a path in at every level."

**Route to:** Consulti.ai (free) → AIA (low commitment) → Insiders (financing) → The Machine (ROI framing)

## Segment Routing Logic

### Entry Point → Default Offer

| Entry Point | Default Offer | Secondary Offer |
|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| AIA opt-in / AI automation content | AIA subscription | Lead Gen Insiders (upsell) |
| Cold email / lead gen content | Lead Gen Insiders | The Machine (upsell) |
| Consulti.ai free trial | Consulti.ai paid tier | Lead Gen Insiders |
| Inbox deliverability content | Inbox Insiders | Lead Gen Insiders |
| High-ticket / DFY interest | The Machine | Lead Gen Insiders (fallback) |
| General list / broad content | Lead Gen Insiders | AIA or Consulti.ai |

---

### Budget Signal Routing

Use behavioral signals, stated budget, or offer page visited to route.

```
IF budget signal = "no money / just starting"
  → Consulti.ai free tier → AIA → mention financing on Insiders

IF budget signal = "under $500"
  → AIA → Lead Gen Insiders (financing angle)

IF budget signal = "$500 – $2,000"
  → Lead Gen Insiders (push lifetime value, $1,497 one-time)

IF budget signal = "$2,000+"
  → Lead Gen Insiders + The Machine (anchor high-ticket)

IF budget signal = "agency / established business"
  → The Machine → schedule call or no-call video
```

### Engagement-Based Routing

| Behavior | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Opens 3+ emails, no click | Send a pattern-interrupt / direct ask email |
| Clicks cold email content | Route to Lead Gen Insiders sequence |
| Clicks AI/automation content | Route to AIA sequence |
| Clicks high-ticket CTA but no purchase | Send The Machine no-call video + FAQ email |
| Purchases Insiders | Introduce The Machine as done-for-you upgrade |
| Purchases AIA | Cross-sell Consulti.ai leads and Inbox Insiders deliverability |
| Purchases Consulti.ai | Upsell Lead Gen Insiders for the full system |
| Clicks but bounces checkout | Enroll in the abandoned-cart / cart-recovery sequence (1hr -> 1d -> 3d). See [sequence-templates.md](sequence-templates.md). |
| 30+ days inactive | Re-engagement with proof email (case study) + low-barrier CTA |

---

### Offer Ladder (Low to High Commitment)

Use this to move subscribers up the value ladder over time.

```
Consulti.ai (free)
  → AIA (low monthly)
    → Lead Gen Insiders ($1,497 one-time / $97/mo financing)
      → Inbox Insiders (infrastructure add-on)
        → The Machine ($7,500–$10,000 DFY)
```

Do not pitch The Machine cold to new subscribers. Build trust with content → case studies → Insiders → DFY pitch.
